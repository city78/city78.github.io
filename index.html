About Us

WE UNDERSTAND THAT THE LIVING ENVIRONMENT  structures the kind of life urban dwellers live, this is why we are committed to structuring the living environment for enhanced urban economic productivity and increased quality of life. City78 aims to bring life to existing and new feedback channels and help organizations understand the interaction between the users of space and the living environment. This insight is essential for identifying existing opportunities and strengths for enhanced urban economic growth.  

WE ARE A TEAM of urban planners, architects, coders and listeners of the urban space. We see the ever-increasing sources of rich community-driven data, combined with traditional government and open data, as an opportunity to solve old, yet ever-present urban problems. We believe that by building a city around solutions derived from understanding the interplay between people and space, their histories, existing perceptions and use of space, we can foster economically sustainable cities.

Our Services

Urban Perception Survey

The best survey your organization never knew it had. As of September 2017, over two-thirds of the world's population is now connected by mobile devices. As this number continues to grow, more and more citizens, tourists and users of the urban space will become intricately connected to the environments they inhabit through their phones.

City78 is the first organization ever to comprehensively survey and analyze entire cities through user-generated data registered on open data and review platforms. Using a mix of traditional urban planning and research techniques along with geospatial clustering algorithms and NLP tools, we bring together the best analytical practices from the urban planning and technology fields to create urban analysis reports through in-depth analysis of data a granular level. 

We are able to track community perception of themes experts care about; walkability, public transportation, safety, parking, atmosphere, scenery and history as well as specific topics like tracking homelessness or cases of racism in commercial and public spaces. 

Through our methods of data-driven community and spatial listening, we are able to identify and cluster narratives individuals hold about the cities they inhabit, develop nuanced recommendations based on the public's input, and foresee and mitigate risks identified by the community, long before other methods of urban analyses are able to.

Our methods of evaluating the urban space reconceptualize participatory planning and co-creation. By directly tapping into organic and unprompted user-generated data, City78 breathes life into the city's feedback channels and helps organizations understand the communities around them, both those that are mapped as well as unmapped, yet have organically mapped themselves. 

Urban Analysis
storeRetail Marketplace Analysis

City78’s Retail Marketplace Analysis recognizes the potential that exists in an urban market for specific commercial uses. This is achieved by measuring existing saturation in current locations, as well as market potential. This cumulates to the identification existing market retail leakage or surplus.

supervisor_accountUser Perception & Place Identity Management

City78 prides itself in its participatory planning approach to understanding communities operating in the urban environment. Through a set of custom-built scripts which leverage built environment user generated data, this model ensures the users of space are actively involved in urban development and the formulation of place identity. This process ensures the creation of organic place identities which in turn ensures competitive advantage for urban areas.

bubble_chartEconomic Clustering

Through advanced and in-depth demographic and marketplace analysis, City78 is able to identify existing and emerging economic clusters. These clusters are characterized by a concentration of similar uses and skilled professionals in close proximity to each other.

my_locationLocation Advantage

By combining multiple analysis methods, City78 arrives at recommendations for the location of office, residential and commercial uses. These recommendations are configured to ensure location advantage by reducing risks and improving long-term economic viability of proposed developments.

location_cityUrban Design Solutions

Using the results of our unique data analysis of an area, we can create schematic designs to meet your needs. We use industry standard (cutting-edge) modeling software to deliver drawings and renderings of potential urban designs, with LEED certification in mind.

Using the Projective Land Use and Demographic Forecast models, City78 prescribes and forecasts future land use mixes that ensure improved urban productivity and quality of life. Ethics

Overview

City78 operates as a for-profit, independent urban analytics agency in the greater Washington DC area. Our work reflects the highest standards in urban planning and data science ethics. City78 works with a variety of clients in government, non-profits, foundations and the private sector. We believe it is necessary to have a diverse client base so that we are able to tell a diversity of stories  as well as ensuring a single funder does not influence the majority of our work.

Client

Just as we are transparent with our data, our client work is met with the same ethical standards and honesty. With each project, we ensure that there is no conflict of interest or perceived conflict of interest. New clients are vetted and accepted by City78 based on their work to create social good. We look at their prior work, financials and use our network to ensure they meet the highest standards of their field before we enter a partnership.

Data

City78 is founded upon openness and transparency. In a time when personal data is leveraged by corporations for micro-targeted ads, we make it a priority to use data for social good. Embedded in our methodological approach to understanding cities is a belief that that economically sustainable communities and cities are ones that foster equitable and just opportunities for all their inhabitants. Therefore we prioritize honesty in our data analysis and reporting regardless of the client. We publish all review and GIS data collected during the course of a project on data.world and Esri ArcGis Online for public sharing and reuse.
