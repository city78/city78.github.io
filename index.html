We listen, not prescribe

Our approach is a paradigm shift away from practices that prescribe urban solutions based on descriptive characteristics of a population. Instead, we listen to communities, involving everyone as a stakeholder in the urban environment to inform and drive urban design. 

City78 has developed our tools and methodologies specifically around place image analysis and organic place identity development models in order to make residents and users of space our co-creators in building ethical, sustainable and resilient cities for the future.

About Us

JUST AS AN ORGANIC CITY is only as effective as its feedback channels, an organization is only as competative as their understanding of the communities they operate in is. City78 aims to bring life to these feedback channels and help organizations understand the communities around them, both those that are mapped as well as those that are unmapped yet have organically mapped themselves, through data-driven community and spatial listening. 

WE ARE A TEAM of planners, coders and listeners of the urban space. We see the ever-increasing sources of rich community-driven data, combined with traditional government and open data, as an opportunity to solve old, yet ever-present urban problems. We believe that by building a city around solutions derived from understanding communities, their histories, needs, perceptions and use of space, we can foster equitable, economically sustainable and resiliant cities.

Our Services

Partner Solutions

City78 helps companies, foundations and nonprofits understand the urban environment through cutting edge urban planning analysis techniques and narrative landscapes mapped across neighborhoods and cities. We use these tools and forward-thinking methodologies to understand the communities residing in the urban space and tell compelling stories through analysis of the narratives they hold. Our team is able to deliver these stories in a number of mediums, from reports to developing websites and interactive visualizations geared toward public viewing.

Data Provision & Visualization

City78 specializes in data collection from a variety of sources, including Esri, Google, Yelp, government Census and open data. We leverage this data to uncover trends, clusters and patterns to develop nuanced analyses of the essence of a city and the communities that it is comprised of. We seek to bring the users of our data in through interactive visualizations using ArcGIS, Tableau and D3. Through these interactive visualizations, we are able to curate an exploratory experience, bringing our viewers through nuanced urban issues to ultimately help them ask the right questions in developing actionable solutions.

 

Project Monitoring

While cities and organizations often spend large amounts prior to implementing new initiatives or development projects in order to understand the area of study, certain projects are difficult to effectively track the progress of their deployment. Using review data, City78 is able to track deployments of new services such as expansions to public transporation infrastructure, new health clinics, as well as monitor consumer reaction to new store and restaurant openings throughout the world. We have developed comprehensive scripts that leverage multiple APIs including Google, Yelp and Dianping to monitor our partners' most important launches.

Research & Recommendations

Through a mix of data science and urban planning, City78 is able to create cluster maps tracking areas of high commercial traffic, clusters of locations with complementary uses, social and citywide issues and test correlations with demographic, zoning and other data quantifying the built environment. These findings can be used to select parcels for future developments, test the economic sustainability and community reception of prospective developments, or assess various qualities of a city for best practice models as well as areas for improvement.

Ethics

Overview

City78 operates as a for-profit, independent urban analytics agency in the greater Washington DC area. Our work reflects the highest standards in urban planning and data science ethics. City78 works with a variety of clients in government, non-profits, foundations and the private sector. We believe it is necessary to have a diverse client base so that we are able to tell a diversity of stories  as well as ensuring a single funder does not influence the majority of our work.

Client

Just as we are transparent with our data, our client work is met with the same ethical standards and honesty. With each project, we ensure that there is no conflict of interest or perceived conflict of interest. New clients are vetted and accepted by City78 based on their work to create social good. We look at their prior work, financials and use our network to ensure they meet the highest standards of their field before we enter a partnership.

Data

City78 is founded upon openness and transparency. In a time when personal data is leveraged by corporations for micro-targeted ads, we make it a priority to use data for social good. Embedded in our methodological approach to understanding cities is a belief that that economically sustainable communities and cities are ones that foster equitable and just opportunities for all their inhabitants. Therefore we prioritize honesty in our data analysis and reporting regardless of the client. We publish all review data collected during the course of a project on data.world for public sharing and reuse.
